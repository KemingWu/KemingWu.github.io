Biography

I am currently a final year MPhil Student in Xiamen University, supervised by Prof. Yue Huang and Prof. Xinghao Ding. Before that, I got the B.Eng degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology at Xiamen University in 2017.

My research spans machine learning and computer vision, with a special interest in transfer learning and domain adaptation.
